# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
AgroDex is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone—regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

We expect all contributors, maintainers, and community members to follow this Code of Conduct.

## Our Standards
We will not tolerate harassment, discrimination, hateful conduct, or harmful behavior.

All participants must:
- Be respectful and professional in communication.
- Treat others fairly and without bias.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Be mindful of different communication styles and language differences.

## Unacceptable Behavior
Examples of unacceptable behavior include (but are not limited to):
- Harassment, stalking, intimidation, or threats.
- Discriminatory comments, slurs, or hateful imagery.
- Sexual content or unwanted attention.
- Abusive language, trolling, or hostility.
- Encouraging harm or illegal activity.
- Publishing or sharing someone else’s private information.



## Enforcement
Violation of this Code of Conduct may result in:
- Warning from maintainers
- Temporary or permanent removal from the community
- Repository access restrictions

Decisions are made by the maintainers to protect the community. We may not be able to provide detailed outcomes, but we will act on reports in a timely manner.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and community channels
- Any project-related events or communications

## Attribution
This document is based on widely used community Code of Conduct patterns and is intended to be straightforward, enforceable, and inclusive.
